Help Desk Page

 

Signing Up

To Sign up, go to the Sign-Up button next to the Log In button under the Log in page. Register your own unique Username (e.g Name01), Email (e.g MyEmail@gmail.com), Password (e.g P@ss11), and Birthdate (01/30/2000) then click on the Sign-up button once you’re finished!

 

Logging In

To log in into your SpringStep account, please make sure to register! Input your registered Username and password then proceed to the Log In button below.

 

Adding a Task

To add a new task, go to the ••• icon beside the “To Do”, “Doing” or “Done” list. After clicking the ••• icon a dropdown menu will show. Click       “Add Task” to open the Task Details window. Input your task title, description, tags, due date and time, and the expected duration of your work time. Click the Save button to create your task.

 

Sort your Tasks

To sort your task according to alphabetical, recently added, or by due date and time, go to the ••• icon beside the “To Do”, “Doing” or “Done” list. A drop-down menu will show then click on the “Sort by…”.

 

Clear a list

To clear all tasks from a list, go to the ••• icon beside the “To Do”, “Doing” or “Done” list. Click on “Clear list” then it will automatically delete all tasks in that specified list.

 

Moving a Task

To move your task, simply drag and drop a task into the “To Do”, “Doing” or “Done” list. Another option is to click on the ••• icon beside the date and time of your task then a drop-down menu will show. Simply click on “Move task to…” and choose which list on where to move your task. By default, SpringStep starts the work timer when you drag a task from the "To Do" list to the “Doing” list.

Edit Task Title

To edit a task title, click on the pencil icon beside the task title. Input your new title and click Enter to confirm or save.

 

Edit a Task

To edit your task, go to the ••• icon beside the date and time of your task then a drop-down menu will show. Click on “Edit Task” and the task details will show. Here you can edit the title, description, tags, due date and time, and expected duration. Once you are finished, click Save.

 

Delete a Task

To delete your task, go to the ••• icon beside the date and time of your task then a drop-down menu will show. Click on Delete task. When clicked, the task will automatically delete itself from your task board.

 

Find a Task

When finding a specific task, go to the search bar located above. Inside the search bar, type the title of the task you want to find, and a drop down will show and indicate which task board it is located. To simple go to the task itself, click on the “jump to…” beside the task title.

 

Creating a Task Board

To create a new task board, go the drop-down icon beside the Task Board title which is located above the search bar. Simply click on “Add new task board” in the drop-down menu. A window will appear in which you will input the task board title in. When you are done, click on Done.

 

Start, Pause, Reset the Timer

By default, SpringStep starts the work timer when you drag a task from the "To Do" list to the “Doing” list. However, if you want to start the timer by itself, click on the buttons below the timer. Start button starts the timer countdown, Pause button halts the timer from counting down, and Reset button sets the timer back to 0 or default. Keep in mind that when the timer has finished its countdown the user will get notification indicating that it is time for their break. When the break time has also finished then the user will get another notification indicating it’s time to work again on their task. The timer follows the Pomodoro technique which is 25 minutes of work time and 5 minutes of break time.

 

Edit or Update User Profile

Click on the icon located on the upper right corner. This will take you into your user profile. Inside the user profile, you can see your profile picture or a default photo, username, email, hidden password, and confirm password. To start editing, click on Edit Profile button. A pencil icon will appear beside your profile picture, username, email, and password. Clicking the pencil icon will open a new window in which you can now edit, upload, and update your user profile. Once you are finished, simply click on Done to save changes.
